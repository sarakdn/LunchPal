About

LUNCHPAL helps you in finding people to talk about your interests while enjoying a meal.

It's not a second Facebook, here you don't collect virtual friends. You only choose a date, a time, a location and some interests you want to talk about during your lunchtime. LUNCHPAL takes care of the rest. It finds your lunchpals who want to talk about the same interests while enjoying their lunch with you.

Aside from being able to edit your own profile, LUNCHPAL allows you to always keep an eye on your planned meetups. LUNCHPAL focuses on the matching with other people for your lunchtime, so there is no management of groups or friends.
